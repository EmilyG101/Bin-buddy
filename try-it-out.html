Recyclable Materials

Plastic Bottles

Rinse them and remove caps before placing them in your building or curbside recycling bin.

Aluminum Cans

Empty and recycle in metal bins or return for deposit at grocery stores.

Metal Cans

Rinse food cans and place in designated metal recycling bins.

Glass Bottles

Recycle using city-provided bins or take to a bottle redemption center.

Cardboard

Flatten boxes and recycle with paper/cardboard waste.

Items That Need Special Disposal

Batteries

Do not throw in trash. Take to battery drop-off at stores like Best Buy or Home Depot.

Electronics

Recycle at electronics events or take to Staples/e-waste centers.

Light Bulbs

CFLs and LEDs should be brought to hardware store collection programs.

Paints/Chemicals

Drop off at your local hazardous waste disposal center.

Plastic Bags

Take to in-store bag recycling bins at supermarkets or department stores.
